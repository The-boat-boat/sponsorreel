About the Business

We are a family-owned brewery serving the downtown harbor district with award-winning craft beers and seasonal ales. At The Rusty Anchor, we believe in community first, good times second, and great beer always. Our taproom hosts weekly trivia nights and supports local artists.

We are looking to expand our brand awareness among local families and young professionals through outdoor cinema events and community gatherings. We love partnering with operators who bring unique experiences to our neighborhood.
